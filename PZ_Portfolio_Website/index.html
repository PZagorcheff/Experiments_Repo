About me

I’m a web developer with a strong foundation in IT and software development, now relaunching my career with fresh skills and hands-on experience. My journey started with a Bachelor’s and Master’s degree in IT, followed by work as a freelance web developer, building websites for clients and sharpening my coding skills.

From there, I moved into software quality assurance in a fintech company, gaining a deep understanding of testing, debugging, and the importance of well-structured code. After a career break while I was working at Amazon UK, where I developed strong problem-solving and teamwork skills, I decided to fully commit to web development again.

To get back on track, I successfully completed a Web Developer Professional Program at Pitman Academy UK, refreshing and expanding my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks. Now, I’m eager to join a team where I can contribute my technical expertise, problem-solving mindset, and attention to detail.

Beyond coding, I enjoy photography, an active lifestyle, and traveling with my family and friends.

Check out my portfolio to see my work. If you’re looking for a reliable and motivated web developer, let’s chat!
